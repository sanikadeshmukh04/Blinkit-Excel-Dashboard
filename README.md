# Blinkit-Grocery-Data-Analysis(Interactive Dashboard Creation using MS Excel)

## Project objective
To analyze grocery sales data and generate insights on sales performance, customer behavior, and outlet characteristics using key metrics and visual dashboards.

## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bKY8BXqaYwGrxBrJDp13jXndhVmIXxFh/edit?gid=2068654923#gid=2068654923">Blinkit Glocary Data</a>

## Key Performance Indicator (KPI) Questions
This project focuses on analyzing key business metrics using data-driven insights. Below are the core KPI questions addressed:

🔹 General Business KPIs

What are the primary KPIs used to measure business performance?
Are current KPIs aligned with business goals?
How frequently should KPIs be monitored?

🔹 Performance Analysis

Are we meeting our KPI targets?
What are the trends observed over time?
What factors are impacting KPI performance?

🔹 Sales KPIs

What is the total revenue generated?
What is the conversion rate of leads to customers?
Which products or regions perform the best?

🔹 Customer KPIs

What is the customer retention rate?
What is the churn rate?
What is the customer satisfaction score (CSAT/NPS)?

🔹 Marketing KPIs

What is the return on investment (ROI) for campaigns?
Which channels generate the most traffic?
What is the cost per lead (CPL)?

🔹 Operational KPIs

What is the process efficiency rate?
What is the average turnaround time?

What is the defect or error rate?
